We Built This for Businesses Like Yours

Most businesses know social media matters. Almost none have the time or systems to do it well. PoppyPages exists to fix that.

The Problem We Kept Seeing

Good businesses — the kind that actually care about their customers — were getting buried online. Not because they did bad work. Because they didn't have time to post about the work they were doing.

Their competitors with worse service but better Instagram were winning the first impression. Potential customers would check a business's social media, see the last post was from 6 months ago, and assume they were closed or unreliable.

That's not fair. So we built something to fix it.

How We Work

1. We Learn Your Business

We start with a free audit of your current online presence. We look at your social channels, Google reviews, website, and competitors. Then we build a plan specific to your business — not a generic template.

2. We Build Your System

You get a monthly content calendar tailored to your goals. We write every post in your voice, design the visuals, and schedule everything. You review and approve — that's your only job.

3. We Keep It Running

We monitor your reviews, respond to comments, track performance, and adjust the strategy monthly. Your online presence stays active and professional without you thinking about it.
