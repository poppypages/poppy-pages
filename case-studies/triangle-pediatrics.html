Triangle Pediatrics

Automating reputation management and local dominance for a high-volume pediatric clinic.

The Challenge

Triangle Pediatrics provides exceptional care, but relying exclusively on front-desk staff to monitor their sprawling digital reputation wasn't scalable. They needed a system that would allow them to act fast on patient feedback without dedicating hours of medical admin time to refreshing a Google Business Profile.

Our Strategy

  1. Automated Review Monitoring: We built an automated workflow that checks their Google Business Profile daily and cross-references it with a spreadsheet of active reviews to immediately flag missing responses or negative feedback.
  2. Drafted Neutralization: By implementing crisis-response protocols, any negative comment is instantly countered with an empathetic, HIPAA-compliant response draft sent directly to their office manager for 1-click approval.
  3. Proactive Brand Publishing: We took over content creation, shifting their social feeds to highly professional graphics that communicated clinic updates efficiently.

The Results

100% Response Rate on New Reviews
20+ Admin Hours Saved Monthly
0 Missed Negative Interactions
