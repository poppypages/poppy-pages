How much should a small business spend on digital marketing?

As a general rule, a small business should target 7-8% of gross revenue for marketing if you make less than $5M a year and your margins are in the 10-12% range. At PoppyPages, our packages start at just $485/mo for high-impact social media management, ensuring you get professional support without blowing the budget.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a marathon, not a sprint. While some local optimization tweaks (like updating your Google Business Profile) can yield results in a matter of weeks, establishing deep topical authority and ranking for competitive keywords typically takes 3 to 6 months of consistent effort.

Why is custom web design better than templates?

Templates use bloated code which makes your site load slowly on mobile, causing users to bounce. A custom-built site is optimized exactly for your content, loads instantly, is dramatically more secure against hacks, and allows for 100% flexibility in design aesthetics to wow your visitors.

Do you handle the actual posting, or just the planning?

We do it all. We write the copy, design the graphics, schedule the posts, and actively publish them to your platforms. You just review a clean 30-day calendar document once a month to give us a thumbs up.
